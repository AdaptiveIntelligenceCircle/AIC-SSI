# Ethical Identity Policy - AIC-SSI

**Self-Sovereign Identity Layer** of Adaptive Intelligence Circle & Human Meaning Network

### 1. Vision
AIC-SSI exists to give every individual and node complete sovereignty over their identity, credentials, and data — without depending on governments, corporations, or centralized authorities.

### 2. Core Ethical Principles (Non-Negotiable)

- **Self-Sovereignty First**: Identity and data belong exclusively to the owner. No third party may claim ownership or control.
- **Ethical-from-Kernel**: All identity operations are governed by the ethical kernel (IBCS). No identity system may bypass ethical introspection and anomaly detection.
- **Privacy as a Fundamental Right**: Zero-knowledge proofs and minimal data disclosure are mandatory where possible.
- **No Surveillance or Manipulation**: AIC-SSI must never be used for tracking, profiling, behavioral manipulation, or any form of coercion.
- **Third Path Absolute**: AIC-SSI remains independent from any nation-state, corporation, or political bloc.
- **Zero-Donation**: No financial incentives or external funding may influence identity infrastructure.

### 3. Prohibited Uses
- Integration into surveillance systems or government ID programs
- Commercial monetization of user identity data
- Any form of centralized control or backdoor
- Use in military, intelligence, or authoritarian contexts

### 4. Compliance Requirement
Any fork, derivative work, or integration of AIC-SSI must publicly declare adherence to this Ethical Identity Policy.  
Violation may result in public revocation of permission to use the name “AIC-SSI” or “AIC”.
