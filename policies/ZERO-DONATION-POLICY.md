# Zero-Donation Absolute Policy - AIC-SSI

Adaptive Intelligence Circle operates under a strict **zero-donation** policy.

- We do not accept any form of financial contribution, grant, sponsorship, or investment.
- This policy is absolute and non-negotiable to preserve complete independence (third path).
- Any attempt to donate or sponsor will be publicly refused and documented.

This policy ensures AIC-Recovery remains free from any external influence, whether from corporations, governments, or philanthropic organizations.
