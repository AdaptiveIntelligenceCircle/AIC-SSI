# Governance Model - AIC-SSI

**Self-Sovereign Identity Layer** of Adaptive Intelligence Circle & Human Meaning Network

### 1. Philosophy
AIC-SSI is governed by the principle that **identity must be sovereign**. No single entity — including the Founder — may claim permanent central control.

### 2. Decision-Making Structure
- **Founder (Nguyễn Đức Trí)** holds final authority on vision, ethical direction, and core architecture.
- **Core Maintainers** (invited by Founder) handle day-to-day development and security.
- **Community Contributors** can propose changes through Pull Requests.
- During the Founder’s mandatory military service (2027–2029), the project enters **maintenance mode**. Only bug fixes, documentation, and security updates are allowed.

### 3. Key Rules
- All major changes to identity architecture must preserve self-sovereignty and ethical-from-kernel.
- No decision may compromise third path absolute or zero-donation principles.
- Any governance change requires public documentation in this repository.

**This model is binding and publicly auditable.**
